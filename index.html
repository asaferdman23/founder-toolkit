Question:
/cofounder-roi Our target: B2B SaaS Pricing: $1,500/month CAC: $2,000 Avg. customer lifetime: 18 months What's our payback period and LTV:CAC ratio?
Response includes:
Payback Period: 1.3 months LTV (18 months): $27,000 CAC: $2,000 LTV:CAC Ratio: 13.5:1 ✓ Healthy Assumptions: - Monthly churn: 5.5% - No expansion revenue - No discounting Interpretation: You recover customer acquisition cost in 1.3 months, leaving 16.7 months of pure profit. Your ratio of 13.5:1 is exceptional for B2B SaaS (healthy = 3:1+). Next Actions: - Invest more in customer acquisition - Target 10% payback or better - Monitor churn — even 1% increase → 5mo payback
