# Founder Toolkit — Your AI Co-Founder Suite

Your personal AI co-founder for **unit economics, market strategy, pitch prep, GTM, investor relations, cost optimization, and social marketing**.

Built for founders, solopreneurs, and product builders who think in numbers.

## Install

```bash
/plugin marketplace add asaferdman23/founder-toolkit
```

Then use any of these commands:

```
/cofounder        → Business strategy router (ROI, pricing, market, GTM, pitch)
/cofounder-roi    → Unit economics, CAC/LTV, break-even, runway, pricing scenarios
/cofounder-market → Segment analysis, market sizing, competitor intel
/cofounder-gtm    → Go-to-market strategy, networking, LinkedIn, conferences
/cofounder-pitch  → Meeting/pitch prep, objection handling, scripts
/investor         → Pitch decks, financial projections, fundraising
/cost-reducer     → Infrastructure costs, API spend optimization
/social-marketing → Social media campaigns, content calendars, ad copy
```

## Skills

### 🎯 **Cofounder** (Router)
Your main entry point. Routes any business question to the right specialist.
- Asks clarifying questions to disambiguate
- Answers general business questions directly (legal, hiring, fundraising, partnerships, equity, etc.)
- Routes to specialized sub-skills for deep dives

**Try:** `/cofounder what should our pricing be?`

---

### 📊 **ROI Calculator**
Calculate unit economics, stress-test pricing, model cash flow, and answer "what if?" scenarios.
- CAC / LTV analysis
- Break-even and runway calculations
- Pricing sensitivity analysis
- Client ROI models
- All outputs as structured tables with clear assumptions

**Try:** `/cofounder-roi what happens if we charge $499/month?`

---

### 🔍 **Market Intel**
Segment your market, size opportunities, understand competitors, and set strategic pricing.
- Customer segment analysis and scoring
- Total addressable market (TAM) sizing
- Competitive landscape and positioning
- Pricing strategy and willingness-to-pay
- Go-to-market channel prioritization

**Try:** `/cofounder-market who should we target first?`

---

### 🚀 **GTM Playbook**
Plan your go-to-market strategy — how to get known, build your network, and create channels.
- Networking strategy and event selection
- LinkedIn playbook and content themes
- Conference strategy and pitch preparation
- Content strategy for growth and awareness
- Partnerships and strategic alliances
- Cold outreach and inbound marketing

**Try:** `/cofounder-gtm how do I get my first 10 customers?`

---

### 🎤 **Pitch Prep**
Prepare for any important meeting — investor pitch, client discovery, demo, board meeting.
- Client research and decision-maker profiling
- ROI arguments tailored to the buyer
- Objection handling and response scripts
- Pricing proposals and value positioning
- Full meeting script with openings, key points, closes
- Follow-up strategy

**Try:** `/cofounder-pitch I have a meeting with an investor next week`

---

### 💰 **Investor Agent**
Prepare materials for fundraising — pitch decks, materials, financial projections, strategy.
- Pitch deck structure and talking points
- Financial model templates and projections
- Fundraising strategy and investor outreach
- Valuation and term sheet analysis
- Competitive positioning for investors
- Market sizing and TAM analysis

**Try:** `/investor I want to raise a seed round`

---

### 💸 **Cost Reducer**
Review spending, optimize API costs, and reduce your cloud bills before they explode.
- Infrastructure cost analysis
- API spend optimization (OpenAI, Twilio, Stripe, etc.)
- Database and storage optimization
- Vendor negotiation and procurement strategy
- Cost-benefit analysis for new services

**Try:** `/cost-reducer my OpenAI bills are out of control`

---

### 📱 **Social Marketing**
Plan campaigns, write viral posts, build content calendars, and grow your audience.
- Content calendar planning (monthly, weekly)
- Post writing for LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok
- Ad copy and campaign strategy
- Growth hacking and audience building
- Brand voice and messaging consistency
- Analytics and performance tracking

**Try:** `/social-marketing plan my LinkedIn content for March`

---

## How It Works

1. **Use the right command** — Start with `/cofounder` for any question, or go directly to a specialist if you know what you need
2. **Answer follow-ups** — Skills ask clarifying questions to understand your situation
3. **Get structured output** — Tables, scenarios, action items, and scripts
4. **Save context** — Skills remember your business metrics, founder cost, and decisions across conversations

## Examples

**Unit Economics:**
```
/cofounder-roi
Our pricing: $999/month
Customer acquisition cost: $500
Average customer lifetime: 24 months
What's the payback period and LTV:CAC ratio?
```

**GTM Strategy:**
```
/cofounder-gtm
B2B SaaS for HR managers
Budget: $500/month
How do I get meetings in the first 90 days?
```

**Pitch Prep:**
```
/cofounder-pitch
Meeting with a Sequoia partner on Thursday
They're interested in our efficiency play
What should I lead with?
```

## Pro Tips

- **Memory:** Skills save assumptions (your hourly rate, real costs, customer data) so they improve with use
- **Routing:** `/cofounder` intelligently routes based on your question — no need to memorize sub-commands
- **Specialist mode:** Go directly to `/cofounder-roi`, `/cofounder-market`, etc. if you know what you need
- **Context:** Save business numbers in memory so you don't repeat them every conversation
