# Pitch Narrative

## The Story Arc

### 1. The Hook (10 seconds)
> "Every corporate event in Israel goes through a producer who charges 40% markup — and companies have zero visibility into what things actually cost. We're fixing that with AI."

### 2. The Problem (60 seconds)

**For HR/Corporate planners:**
- Producer charges NIS 350K for an event that costs NIS 250K in real supplier costs
- Zero cost transparency — you never see the real numbers
- Finding suppliers takes 1.5-3 months (Instagram scrolling, WhatsApp, word-of-mouth)
- Every event starts from scratch — no data, no structure, no learning
- Bad vendor picks discovered on event day, not in planning phase
- Scattered across Excel, WhatsApp, email, Google Drive

**Validated pain quotes:**
- Maya Dan (Checkpoint): "I pay NIS 350K but have no idea what the real costs are"
- Lihi (Intel HR): "It takes me 1.5-3 months just to find the right suppliers"
- 5/5 producers confirmed they markup 30-50% on every supplier

### 3. The Solution (90 seconds)

**ProduceGerX = AI Event Architect + Vetted Supplier Network + Producer Backup**

1. **Describe your event** → AI generates full plan: vendors, budget, timeline, concept visuals
2. **Browse vetted suppliers** → Categorized by specialization (corporate photographer ≠ wedding photographer), with portfolios, pricing, reviews
3. **Full cost transparency** → Side-by-side comparison: "Producer charges X, you pay Y"
4. **One central hub** → Quotes, contracts, payments, timelines, communication — all in one place
5. **Safety net** → Real producer on standby if you get stuck

### 4. The "Why Now" (30 seconds)
- AI makes it possible to replace the producer's knowledge (GPT-4 + DALL-E for concept visualization)
- Post-COVID corporate events spending is back to peak levels
- Remote/hybrid work = more intentional team gatherings (HR budgets growing)
- Gen Z office managers expect self-service tools, not phone calls
- WhatsApp Business API enables direct supplier communication at scale

### 5. Traction (30 seconds)
- Product fully built (10-step event management journey)
- AI Event Architect generating concept images + vendor matches
- WhatsApp supplier onboarding bot live
- 27/50 pilot spots committed
- Demo scheduled with Checkpoint Cyber Security (March 16, 2026)
- Bilingual (Hebrew + English) — ready for Israeli market

### 6. Business Model (30 seconds)
- **SaaS subscription** — Monthly fee per organization (tiered by event volume)
- **Transaction fee** — % on supplier bookings made through platform
- **Premium features** — AI concept generation, floor plan editor, PDF/PPTX export
- **Phase 2** — Producer lead marketplace (producers pay for qualified HR leads)

### 7. The Ask
- **Pre-seed:** $500K-$1M for 12-18 months runway
- **Use of funds:** 60% product + engineering, 25% sales + marketing, 15% operations
- **Milestones:** 10 paying customers, $50K MRR, expand to 3 vertical markets

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Producers will fight you" | We're not selling to producers. We're selling to their customers. Producers become Phase 2 lead marketplace. |
| "Events are relationship-driven" | Exactly — that's why we have vetted suppliers with portfolios, not just listings. And a real producer backup. |
| "Marketplace chicken-and-egg" | We onboard suppliers via WhatsApp bot (automated), and we start with curated supply (50 vetted vendors). HR demand drives itself once they see cost savings. |
| "What about WellBe?" | WellBe handles simple happy hours. We target complex corporate events (NIS 100K-500K) — completely different segment. |
| "How do you ensure quality?" | Deep categorization (not just "photographer" — corporate/wedding/product), vetting process, reviews, and the producer backup as safety net. |
| "Israel is small" | Israel is the beachhead. Corporate events are universal. The AI + workflow translates globally. |
| "Why can't Wix/Monday do this?" | They're horizontal tools. We're vertical — event-specific AI, supplier matching, quote comparison, timeline management. Built for the workflow. |
