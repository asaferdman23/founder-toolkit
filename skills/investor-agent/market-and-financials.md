# Market Sizing & Financial Projections

## TAM / SAM / SOM

### TAM — Total Addressable Market
**Global corporate events market:** $500B+ (Allied Market Research)
- Corporate meetings, conferences, team events, offsites

### SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market
**Israeli corporate events:** ~$2B annually
- ~600K registered companies in Israel
- ~50K companies with 50+ employees (event buyers)
- Average 2-4 corporate events/year
- Average spend: NIS 50K-500K per event

### SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market (Year 1-3)
**Target: Tech companies + large corporates in Tel Aviv metro**
- ~5,000 companies with 100+ employees in central Israel
- If 2% adopt in Year 1 = 100 companies
- Average 3 events/year = 300 events
- Average platform spend: NIS 5K/event (subscription + transaction)
- **Year 1 SOM: NIS 1.5M (~$400K)**

### Key Assumptions
- 10% month-over-month customer growth after product-market fit
- 90% annual retention (events are recurring, switching cost is high)
- Transaction fee: 3-5% on supplier bookings through platform
- Average event budget: NIS 150K (platform captures NIS 5K-7.5K)
