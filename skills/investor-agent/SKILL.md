# ProduceGerX Investor Agent

## Overview
Strategic fundraising advisor for ProduceGerX — the AI-powered event infrastructure platform replacing producers for corporate HR teams. Helps craft pitch narratives, financial models, market sizing, and investor materials grounded in real product data and validated customer pain.

## When to Use
- Writing or refining pitch decks
- Preparing for investor meetings
- Building financial projections
- Market sizing and TAM/SAM/SOM calculations
- Competitive analysis
- Crafting the fundraising narrative
- Due diligence preparation

## Company Snapshot

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Product** | AI event management platform (producer replacement) |
| **Target** | Corporate HR / office managers planning events |
| **Pain Solved** | 40%+ producer markup, zero cost transparency, scattered workflows |
| **Moat** | AI planning + vetted supplier network + real producer backup |
| **Stage** | Pre-seed / Seed — product built, first pilot customer (Checkpoint) |
| **Market** | Israeli events industry ($2B+), expandable to global corporate events |
| **Business Model** | B2B SaaS — subscription + transaction fees on supplier bookings |

- **market-and-financials.md** — TAM/SAM/SOM, unit economics, revenue projections
