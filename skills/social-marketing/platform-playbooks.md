# Platform Playbooks

## LinkedIn (Primary B2B Channel)

### Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** "Building the future of corporate events | Founder @ ProduceGerX"
- **Banner:** Product screenshot with "Save 40% on event production"
- **About:** Pain story → solution → traction → CTA
- **Featured:** Pitch deck, product demo video, waitlist link

### Content Strategy
- **Posting frequency:** 4-5x/week
- **Best times (Israel):** Sunday-Thursday, 8-9 AM or 12-1 PM
- **Format mix:** 40% text posts, 30% carousels, 20% polls, 10% video
- **Hashtags:** #EventManagement #HRTech #EventTech #CorporateEvents #אירועים #הפקהעסקית

### Growth Tactics
1. **Comment on HR community posts** — Add value, don't pitch
2. **Tag HR managers** in relevant content (with permission)
3. **Join LinkedIn groups:** Israel HR, Event Professionals, Tech HR
4. **Publish articles** on event industry trends (LinkedIn SEO)
5. **Connection strategy:** 20 targeted connections/day (HR managers at 100+ employee companies)

### Post Templates

**Pain-Point Post:**
```
I talked to an HR manager last week.

She told me her company paid ₪350K for their annual event.
The actual supplier costs? ₪210K.

That's ₪140K in producer markup.

This is why we built ProduceGerX.
Full cost transparency. AI-powered planning. Vetted suppliers.

No more hidden markups.

→ Join 27 other companies on our waitlist: [link]

#EventManagement #CostTransparency #HRTech
```

## Instagram (Visual + Inspiration)

### Profile Setup
- **Bio:** "AI Event Planning for Corporate Teams 🎯 | Save 40% on production costs | Join the waitlist ↓"
- **Link in bio:** Waitlist page with UTM tracking
- **Highlights:** How It Works, Real Savings, Product Demo, Testimonials

### Content Strategy
- **Posting frequency:** 3-4 feed posts/week + daily Stories
- **Best times:** Sunday-Thursday, 11 AM-1 PM or 7-9 PM (Israel)
- **Format mix:** 50% Reels, 30% carousels, 20% single image

### Reel Ideas (High Engagement)
1. **"60-second event plan"** — Screen recording of AI chat generating a full event
2. **"What ₪350K gets you"** — Split screen: producer vs. ProduceGerX
3. **"The spreadsheet graveyard"** — Show chaotic Excel → clean dashboard transition
4. **"Mood board magic"** — DALL-E generating concept images in real-time
5. **"POV: Your HR manager finds out about producer markups"** — Trending audio
6. **"How suppliers see your event"** — Show supplier portal/catalog upload

### Story Strategy
- **Monday:** Poll ("How do you plan corporate events?")
- **Tuesday:** Behind-the-scenes product development
- **Wednesday:** Quick tip carousel
- **Thursday:** User quote/testimonial
- **Friday:** Weekend inspiration (event photos)

## TikTok (Viral Potential)

### Profile
- **Bio:** "Saving companies 40% on event production 💰 | AI-powered event planning"
- **Link:** Waitlist

### Content Strategy
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x/week
- **Format:** Short-form (15-60 sec), hook in first 2 seconds
- **Tone:** More casual, funny, relatable than LinkedIn

### Video Concepts
1. **"Things producers don't want you to know"** — Series format
2. **"HR manager discovers the markup"** — Reaction format
3. **"Planning a 400-person event in 60 seconds with AI"** — Satisfying demo

### Growth Hacks

## Cross-Platform Rules
1. **Repurpose, don't copy** — Same message, adapted for platform
2. **LinkedIn → long text, professional** | **Instagram → visual, aspirational** | **TikTok → short, entertaining**
3. **Track UTMs** on every link (platform, campaign, content type)
4. **Engage before posting** — 15 min engaging with others' content before your post goes live
5. **Respond to DMs within 4 hours** — These are potential leads
