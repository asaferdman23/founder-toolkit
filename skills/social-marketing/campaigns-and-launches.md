# Campaigns & Launches

## Waitlist Campaign (Current — 27/50 spots)

### Urgency Messaging
- "27/50 spots taken — limited pilot access"
- "We're only onboarding 50 companies. Here's why."
- Countdown posts as spots fill (30/50, 40/50, LAST 5)

### Referral Loop
- Every waitlist member gets a unique referral link
- "Refer an HR friend → jump the queue"
- Track with UTM: `?ref={user_id}`

### Email Sequence (Post-Signup)
1. **Day 0:** Welcome + what to expect + "here's what we're building" video
2. **Day 3:** Pain-point article — "The 40% markup nobody talks about"
3. **Day 7:** Product sneak peek (AI Event Architect demo)
4. **Day 14:** Social proof — "Here's what Checkpoint is testing"
5. **Day 21:** Feature highlight — cost comparison tool
6. **Day 30:** "Your spot is confirmed — here's when we launch"

## Feature Launch Playbook

**Use this template for EVERY major feature launch:**

### Week -1 (Teaser)
- LinkedIn: "We've been working on something that'll change how you compare vendor quotes..."
- Instagram Story: Blurred screenshot + "Coming next week"
- Build anticipation, don't reveal

### Launch Day
- **LinkedIn:** Detailed post explaining the problem → how this feature solves it
- **Instagram:** Reel demo (30-60 sec) + carousel explaining benefits
- **TikTok:** Quick "watch this" demo with trending audio
- **Email:** Feature announcement to waitlist
- **Product Hunt** (for major features): "ProduceGerX — AI Event Planning for Corporate Teams"

### Week +1 (Social Proof)
- Share first user reactions
- "Here's what [Company] said about [feature]"
- Tutorial content: "3 ways to use [feature]"

## Paid Campaign Frameworks

### LinkedIn Ads (Primary)

**Campaign 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)**
- **Target:** HR managers, office managers, 100+ employee companies in Israel
- **Ad type:** Single image or carousel
- **Copy:** Lead with the 40% markup stat
- **CTA:** "Learn more" → Landing page
- **Budget:** NIS 100/day test, scale winners

**Campaign 2: Retargeting (Bottom of Funnel)**
- **Target:** Website visitors, landing page viewers
- **Ad type:** Video (product demo)
- **Copy:** "You looked. Here's what 27 companies already signed up for."
- **CTA:** "Join waitlist"
- **Budget:** NIS 50/day

**Ad Copy Templates:**

```
🎯 Your event producer takes 40%.

Every corporate event in Israel goes through a producer
who marks up every supplier by 30-50%.

You'd never know — because the real costs are hidden.

ProduceGerX shows you the real numbers.
AI-powered planning. Vetted suppliers. Full transparency.

→ Join 27 companies already on the waitlist.
```

### Instagram/Facebook Ads

**Best formats:** Reels ads (15 sec), carousel ads
**Target:** HR professionals, office managers, Israel, age 28-45
**Lookalike audience:** Based on waitlist signups (once you have 100+)

## Content Creation Workflow

1. **Sunday:** Plan week's content (topics + formats)
2. **Monday-Tuesday:** Create 3-4 posts for the week
3. **Wednesday:** Review + schedule via Buffer/Later
4. **Daily:** 15 min engaging with audience + respond to DMs
5. **Friday:** Review weekly metrics, note what worked
6. **Monthly:** Full analytics review, adjust strategy
