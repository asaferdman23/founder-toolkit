# Content Strategy

## Content Pillars

### 1. Cost Transparency (40% of content)
**Goal:** Make HR managers question what they're really paying producers

**Post types:**
- "What your producer charges vs. what it actually costs" (infographic)
- Real price breakdowns (anonymized): "NIS 350K event → NIS 250K in real costs"
- "The 40% markup nobody talks about" (carousel)
- Calculator: "How much are you overpaying?" (interactive)

**Example post (Hebrew):**
> הזמנתם הפקה לאירוע של 400 איש?
> המפיק גובה ₪350,000.
> העלות האמיתית של הספקים: ₪210,000.
> ההפרש? ₪140,000 שאתם משלמים על... מה בדיוק?
>
> ProduceGerX מראה לכם את המספרים האמיתיים. הכל שקוף.

### 2. Behind the Scenes (25% of content)
**Goal:** Show the product, build trust, demonstrate capability

**Post types:**
- Product demos (screen recordings with voiceover)
- "Watch our AI plan an event in 60 seconds" (Reels/TikTok)
- Before/after: scattered Excel vs. ProduceGerX dashboard
- Concept image generation showcase (DALL-E mood boards)
- Floor plan editor demo

### 3. Event Horror Stories (20% of content)
**Goal:** Create urgency — bad things happen without proper tools

**Post types:**
- "The photographer who showed up to a corporate event with wedding equipment"
- "When the caterer brought 200 portions for 400 guests" (anonymized real stories)
- "Why 'recommended by a friend' isn't a vetting process"
- Polls: "What's the worst event disaster you've experienced?"

### 4. Thought Leadership (15% of content)
**Goal:** Position founder as event-tech expert

**Post types:**
- Industry trends (AI in events, post-COVID corporate events)
- "5 questions to ask your producer before signing"
- "Why HR departments are taking events in-house"
- Guest posts / podcast appearances recaps

## Post Frameworks

### The "Did You Know" Hook
> Did you know [surprising stat]?
> [2-3 lines expanding the pain]
> [What we do differently]
> [CTA]

### The "Compare" Carousel
> Slide 1: "How you plan events NOW"
> Slide 2-5: Pain points with visuals
> Slide 6: "How it could look"
> Slide 7-10: ProduceGerX solution
> Slide 11: CTA + waitlist link

### The "Real Numbers" Post
> [Specific price breakdown]
> [What each line item actually costs]
> [What the markup is]
> [How ProduceGerX gives transparency]

## Content Rules
1. Every post must have ONE clear message (not three)
2. Lead with Hebrew, add English version for reach
3. Use real numbers whenever possible (anonymized)
4. Always end with a CTA (waitlist, DM, website)
5. Respond to EVERY comment within 2 hours
6. No stock photos — use product screenshots, real events, or DALL-E generated visuals
